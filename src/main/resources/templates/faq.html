Frequently Asked Questions

How To Donate?

Install MetaMask: If you don't already have MetaMask, you can download and install it for free as a browser extension.

Set up your wallet: After installing MetaMask, create a new wallet or connect it to your existing one.

Connect your wallet to our platform: Click "Connect Wallet" on our website. MetaMask will automatically open, allowing you to confirm the details of your wallet.

Input Amount: You will be asked to enter the number of BDC tokens you would like to donate. Once you have entered this, you can click on the Donate button.

Confirm the transaction: Review the transaction details in MetaMask and confirm the payment.

Thank you for your donation!: Your donation will be securely and anonymously sent to the chosen charity project.

How To Create An AD?

Create an Account: To create an Advertisement you must first create an account or log in to your existing account.

Verify yourself: If you have not yet verified yourself, you must upload a verification document and wait for manual verification.

Create an AD: You can start by creating an advert, for which you need a description, wallet ID and, if possible, a picture.

How To Create An Account?

Create an Account: If you don't have an account yet, you can easily create one by clicking on the "Register" navbar item.

Input Data: You will be asked to enter your username and password. Please use a unique username to avoid errors.

Login: To log in to your account you have to click on the navbar item "Login" and enter your previously entered data.

How To SWAP Tokens?

SWAP Tokens: To swap tokens you have to click on the navbar item "Swap".

Connect your wallet to our platform: Click "Connect Wallet" on our website. MetaMask will automatically open, allowing you to confirm the details of your wallet.

Input Amount: You will be asked to enter the number of BDC or ETH you want to use for the swap. After that you can click on the SWAP button.

Confirm the transaction: Review the transaction details in MetaMask and confirm the swap.

Exchanged tokens: The exchanged tokens should then be displayed in your wallet in a few seconds.
